Katie Louise Dixon

I have a background in the arts, having worked in art galleries around the North West throughout my career. In 2015 I got involved at Leigh Hackspace and after building a community darkroom for the space I was invited to join the Board of Directors in 2016.

I’m a STEM ambassador and help people of all ages be creative through Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics. I have primarily worked in analogue photographic techniques but would love to expand my practice into digital arts.

I’m really excited about the new creative potential that's available through the coding skills I’ve gained at CodeNation.
